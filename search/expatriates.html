U.S. Expatriates

For 2019, there aren’t major changes to your benefits. However, there’s a new way to enroll in case you do want to make some changes.

MyDanaherBenefits.com is replacing the MyBenefits.MyDanaher.com site. The names are similar, so make note (and bookmark) the new site to make sure you’re in the right place.

If you want to make changes for 2019, the enrollment period is November 1 – 20, 2018.

  • Go to MyDanaherBenefits.com beginning November 1. Sign in automatically from Danaher Connect. Or enter the same user ID and password on the new site as you use today on MyBenefits.MyDanaher.com.
  • If you do not make any changes, you’ll have the same benefits and coverage levels as you do now, except for Flexible Spending Accounts—you must enroll in these each year.
  • Review your beneficiaries for your life and AD&D insurance plans and update them if you need to.
