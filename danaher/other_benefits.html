Other benefits

As a Danaher associate, you have the opportunity to enroll in extra benefits and programs—all designed to help make your life a little easier. And Annual Enrollment is a great time to consider whether one or more of these programs could work for you. Please note some location-specific benefits may not be listed here. Visit MyDanaherBenefits.com for details.

Adoption Assistance Help with qualified expenses if you’re building a family through adoption
Associate Discounts Discounts on travel (rental cars and hotels), computers, and Chrysler, Ford and GM cars
Auto & Home Protection Plan Special auto and home insurance rates that you can pay for through paycheck contributions
Cigna Healthy Rewards Member Discounts Discounts on products and services including weight management and nutrition, fitness devices and centers, and alternative therapies such as acupuncture
Identity Theft Plan Discounted identity theft coverage through ID Watchdog
Legacy Planning Services Help with creating a legacy plan or final arrangements for active or retired associates and other covered family members
Legal Assistance Plan Access to attorneys who can advise on personal and family matters, at a low group rate
LifeSuite Services Confidential help with legal issues, financial concerns, or managing grief
Pet Insurance Coverage for medical care for your pet, including preventive care and/or comprehensive coverage options
Travel Assist Coverage for accidents and medical needs while traveling on Danaher business
