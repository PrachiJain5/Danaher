Dental and vision

Like your medical plan, dental and vision coverage also includes preventive care benefits. Take advantage of these benefits and schedule regular checkups—and avoid bigger problems down the road.

Dental key features
  • Covers routine checkups and other comprehensive dental services (including orthodontic treatment).
  • Administered by Cigna, the plan pays the full cost of preventive care, including semi-annual exams, cleanings and X-rays.
  • Pay an annual deductible for other services, and then the plan shares the cost with you.
  • Pay less for covered services when you see a Cigna dentist.
  • ID cards available online
  • Note that there’s a replacement teeth exclusion for 24 months if you’re a new hire or newly eligible for benefits.*

    • * If you’re part of a recent acquisition with benefits effective in January 2019, contact the Advocacy team to see if there is a special process for allowing replacement if the teeth pulled and replaced are within an 18-month period.

Vision key features
  • Helps you save money on eye exams, glasses and corrective contact lenses. Vision services are provided through Vision Service Plan (VSP).
  • Use a VSP network provider and get more for your money on eye care services and supplies, and qualify for discounts.
  • No ID cards needed, but you can print one at vsp.com.
  • Pay your copay and the plan pays in-network benefits, and your provider will handle the claims filing.
