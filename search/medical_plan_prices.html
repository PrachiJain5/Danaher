Medical plan prices

There’s an easy way to compare the cost of the plans and carriers you’re thinking about for next year.

The Pricing Modeler is available now to help you compare the cost-per-paycheck of your medical plan options. Use the code that was sent to you via email, or in the printed guide mailed home.*

What you pay depends on:

  • The plan you choose
  • The carrier you choose
  • Who you cover
*Note the Pricing Modeler is not available to associates in Hawaii or U.S. Expatriates.
